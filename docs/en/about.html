What HACK/PROJECT is

HACK/PROJECT publishes a daily analysis of a real cyber attack: origin, attack chain mapped to MITRE ATT&CK, detection and remediation. Every dossier ships in Italian and English, and becomes a vertical video on TikTok.

Method

Every dossier starts from primary sources: CISA, FBI and NCSC advisories, reports from the vendors who ran the incident response, official MITRE pages. Two independent sources minimum. ATT&CK technique IDs are copied from advisories, never inferred.

Where attribution is disputed, where a source stands alone, where a CVSS score hasn't been validated yet: we say so inside the article. A threat intelligence site that fakes certainty it doesn't have is worth less than nothing.

The content is defensive. We describe tactics and techniques at the same level of detail as a public advisory; we don't distribute offensive code or reusable payloads.
